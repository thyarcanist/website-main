Datorien Anderson — Writings

Immersive Simulations: Crafting Complex Interactive Experiences

May 2023

The Asset:

Creating Immersion without Code

What made this project particularly challenging—and interesting—was that it was all achieved without writing a single line of code. Instead, I leveraged the capabilities of Unity, a powerful game development platform, to create intricate state machines and detailed interactions. The result was a deeply immersive and interactive system that could respond to user inputs, cycle through diagnostic testing, and even display error codes in a realistic manner.

Troubleshooting in Real-Time: An Exercise in Problem Solving

As with any complex system, this project presented its share of challenges. From creating a blinking LED effect without access to the proper tools, to the need for an extensive debug menu to fix issues that cropped up on the engineering side, I found myself continually problem-solving and adapting. But through perseverance and a deep understanding of the platform, I was able to address these issues and continue to refine the simulation, creating an even more immersive and interactive experience.

The Takeaway

While this project remained largely unseen during my time at Interplay Learning, it remains a testament to the power of immersive simulation and interactive design. It stands as a symbol of what can be achieved with dedication, creativity, and a deep understanding of both technology and user experience. I'm immensely proud of what I was able to accomplish with this system, and I look forward to the opportunity to bring my skills and experience to bear on new challenges in the future.
